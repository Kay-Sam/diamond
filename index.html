About us

We are leading the future of diamond research through science, precision, and innovation.

At Jean Ann Foley Diamond Institute, our team of scientists and researchers is committed to advancing the understanding of diamonds—from their natural formation to modern applications.

By combining advanced gemological technology with rigorous methodologies, we uncover deeper insights into diamond structure, clarity, and origin. Our commitment to ethical standards ensures every result is accurate, transparent, and industry-relevant.
